ATTENTION!

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When registering an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and other necessary information.
  • Payment Information: For deposits and withdrawals, we collect payment details such as bank account numbers, UPI IDs, or e-wallet information.
  • Technical Data: We collect technical data such as your IP address, device information, and browsing patterns when you use our site or app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your account and facilitate deposits/withdrawals.
  • To communicate with you about updates, promotions, and bonuses.
  • To ensure a safe and secure gaming environment.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including anti-money laundering regulations.

3. Data security

  • We use industry-standard security measures such as encryption and secure socket layers (SSL) to protect your data.
  • Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Data security

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies providing payment processing, customer support, or technical services.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights or the rights of others.

5. Cookies Policy

  • We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. These small text files are stored on your device to help us analyze site usage, remember your preferences, and deliver targeted content.
  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the basic functioning of the site.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us improve user experience by analyzing site traffic.
  • Advertising Cookies: Display relevant ads based on your preferences.

6. Third-Party Links

  • Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information.

7. Your rights

  • Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can correct or update inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data if you wish to close your account.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you via email or through a notice on the website.
